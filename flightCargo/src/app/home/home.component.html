Welcome to Flight Cargo!

We are a new company on the market that operates cargo flights from Bulgaria to Europe.
Our main routes are currently from Bulgaria to Germany (Stuttgart and Berlin) and France (Paris).
We plan to develop and operate more flights to more distant parts of the world.
Soon we will sign a partnership contracts to operate cargo flights to the USA and Canada.

Our responsibility is to transport cargo from airport to airport without any problems.
For now, we have four aircraft adapted for cargo manufactured by the famous companies Boeing, Airbus and McDonnell Douglas.
These aircraft manufacturers have proven themselves over time with their accuracy, loyalty and reliability in order for your cargo to reach unharmed from airport to airport.
